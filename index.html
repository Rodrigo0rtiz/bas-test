✈️ BAS AI Flight Service Assistant

A prototype chatbot for Bellingham Aviation Services that helps customers and pilots get quick answers about common FBO services while flagging urgent or sensitive requests for staff review.

Hello! I’m the BAS AI Flight Service Assistant. I can help with common questions about fuel, parking, rental cars, hotels, after-hours arrivals, deicing, catering, and arrival procedures. For urgent, security-sensitive, or operational requests, I will flag the message for BAS staff review.
