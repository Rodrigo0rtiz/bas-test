✈️ BAS Internal Flight Service Assistant

An internal prototype for Bellingham Aviation Services front desk staff. This assistant helps workers remember procedures, respond to common aviation service questions, and identify when a request needs human judgment or direct follow-up.

Hello! I’m the BAS Internal Flight Service Assistant. I am designed for staff use, not direct customer use. Ask me what to do when a customer asks about fuel, rental cars, after-hours arrivals, hangar space, hotel recommendations, customs, deicing, or student pilot questions. I can provide a staff checklist, a suggested response, and any human-review warnings.

Staff Scenarios

Purpose

This tool is a memory aid and drafting assistant for BAS workers. It helps reduce inconsistent answers and supports newer employees who may not remember every procedure.

Human-in-the-loop

The assistant suggests what staff should check and how they might respond, but employees still make the final decision.

Safety rules

  • Do not automatically release access codes.
  • Flag urgent or sensitive situations.
  • Confirm prices, availability, and procedures before replying.
